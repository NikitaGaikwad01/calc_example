SOLID is an acronym that stands for the following:

Single responsibility principle

Open/closed principle

Liskov substitution principle

Interface segregation principle

Dependency inversion principle

These five principles are not a specific ordered list (do this, then that, etc) but a collection of best practices, developed through the decades. They are gathered into an acronym, as a mnemonic vehicle to be remembered, similar to others in computer science,
